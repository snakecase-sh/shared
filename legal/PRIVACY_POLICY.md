- **OAuth Tokens:**

### 2.2 Message Data

When you use our messaging features, we collect:

- **Message Content:**
  - Text messages you send
  - Timestamps of messages

- **Message Encryption:**
  - All messages are encrypted using AES-256-GCM encryption
  - Encryption keys are derived from your authentication credentials
  - We cannot read the content of your encrypted messages

We automatically collect certain information when you use the Service:

- **Technical Data:**
  - IP address
  - Browser type and version
  - Device type and operating system
  - Time zone setting and location

- **Usage Data:**
  - Pages visited
  - Time spent on pages
  - Links clicked
  - Error logs
  - Performance metrics

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:
- Maintain your session

- Analyze usage patterns

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.
